The About Screen is an additional tab pre-populated for each app. It displays information about your app including links to sending feedback or reports about the app.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/ux/about-screen/about-screen-one.png" caption="About Screen" /%}

The **About Screen** displays the following information about your app:

1. App Author
2. Share Link
3. A link to send feedback to the author
4. A link to report the app

{% callout title="Upgrade to Pro" %}
If your app is upgraded to Pro, you can hide the About Screen in your Sharing settings.
{% /callout %}

## Sending feedback

Users can send feedback regarding your app in the Support section of the screen. The feedback is sent to your email or the app author's email.

![](/docs/reference/ux/about-screen/sending-feedback.gif)

## Reporting the app

Users can also report the app in the Security section of the screen.

![](/docs/reference/ux/about-screen/reporting-the-app.gif)
