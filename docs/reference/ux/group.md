The **Group** option allows you to group your lists according to a specified column found in your data. This gives you the ability to breakdown your lists into specific categories. 

In the example below, we grouped the products in the app according to their brands. 

![](/docs/reference/ux/placeholder-folder/group/grouping-comparison.png)

The Group option works for all the list items in Apps and Collections in Pages. You can choose any column in your data as the category for grouping.
