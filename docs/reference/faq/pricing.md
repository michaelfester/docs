## Do you offer special pricing for non-profits and schools?

Yes, you can [apply here](https://glide.typeform.com/to/PFmvEk0w) for a discounted plan. This plan is only available to accredited non-profits and educational institutions; it is not available for purchase by individuals or students.

## What are boosts?

Boosts were paid addons to increase the capacity of your apps. They are no longer available for purhase.

## How do I make a new copy of a template I've purchased?

Simply copy it from the template store again–if you've already purchased a template, you will not be charged again when you copy it to the same time. You can 'buy' it as many times as you want.
