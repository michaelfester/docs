The Video component displays videos in your project from platforms like YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, and more. To display a video in your project, you just need a video's URL.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/components/video-component/video-com%5Bonent-use-case.png" caption="" /%}

## Data

In the **General** tab on the right side of the Layout Editor, locate the **Data** section. Use the menu in **Video** to select the value from your data to display, or select **Custom** to enter a [Custom Value](/docs/custom-values) for this field.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/components/video-component/video-component-configuration.png" caption="" /%}

{% callout type="warning" title="" %}
Hosting videos for the Video Component in Glide Tables or on Google Drive is not advised. Neither of those cloud storage locations is a video streaming service and may be unreliable. Instead, we encourage you to host your video on a streaming service (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.).
{% /callout %}

### Options

You can customize the **Aspect Ratio** of your video by selecting this option in **General > Options**.
