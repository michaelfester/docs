Intercom is a messaging platform that connects businesses to their customers directly. The most common use case is a chat button on the bottom right of a website or product, where users can chat live with a representative from the company. By connecting Intercom with your Glide app, you can chat with your users in real time. 

![live-view-of-intercom-widget.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/3cedda84-9d18-4140-a191-ea454b3ce366/live-view-of-intercom-widget.png)

# Adding and Configuring the Integration

Setting up the Intercom integration in Glide takes just a few steps:

1. In your Glide project, open the **********Settings********** menu. 
2. In the **Integrations** menu, choose ******************Intercom****************** and select **Add to app**. This will ask for your intercom App ID.
    
    ![choosing-the-intercom-intergration.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/c22819c5-abe7-4924-abfa-21916e6751e2/choosing-the-intercom-intergration.png)
    
3. In Intercom, locate your workspace ID. You can find this in Intercom under settings —> installation —> web and then select **with an integration** and ****************************copy workspace ID****************************. You can choose any integration to show the workspace ID button.
    
    ![copying-the-intercom-workspace-id.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/9b0b9517-d6f4-417b-8190-902d3d9249ee/copying-the-intercom-workspace-id.png)
    
4. Paste the workspace ID into your Glide integrations setup.
    
    ![pasting-the-app-id-in-glide.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/f965c386-708e-4c76-ac13-33b88c4afb76/pasting-the-app-id-in-glide.png)
    
5. Your integration is now connected!

# Features

When you add Intercom to a Glide app, the Intercom overlay widget will automatically appear on the live page that your users interact with.

To preview this, publish your Glide app. If you aren’t ready for the app to be public yet, you can publish in private mode. This is the default setting when you publish a project. 

![sharing-an-app-in-private-mode.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/6a4775b6-ca61-4ab0-b359-7b31b7c1a434/sharing-an-app-in-private-mode.png)

Once you’ve published your project, copy the live app link and view the published version of your app. 

![copying-the-app-link.png](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/7fe622a0-50c7-4c00-a140-2d26415431ab/copying-the-app-link.png)

Here you will see the Intercom widget displayed.

# FAQs

- Which components and actions support the Intercom integration?
    
    The Intercom integration acts as an overlay that sits on top of your published project. It will not be visible to you in the Glide builder. It will only appear on the live, published page of your project. 
    
- I can’t find my Intercom Workspace ID
    
    There are a few places to find the Workspace ID. Visit Intercom’s documentation for more details.
