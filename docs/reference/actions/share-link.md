The Share Link action triggers the device's share panel, enabling them to share your content via other apps.

![](/docs/reference/actions/share-link/Group%2062.png)

## Data

Once you set the Share link action, you can bind your component to share any of the following data:

1. Custom Text
2. Unique values like [Current Date/Time](/docs/current-date-time), a [Unique Identifier](/docs/unique-id), [User's Email Address](/docs/users-email-address), or the [Link to the current screen](/docs/deep-links)
3. Data from a specified column in your sheet
