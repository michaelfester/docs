To add or update your billing information, open the Billing section under your Team folder.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/billing-profile.png" caption="Billing information" /%}

Under Billing Information, type in your company name and on the next section, Additional Information, you are free to input any additional details such as address, phone number, etc.,
You can also add your tax details - Tax Type and Tax ID number.

{% callout title="Billing profiles" %}
Each [team](/docs/teams) has its separate billing profile. You will need to setup a billing profile for each to be able to upgrade your projects.
{% /callout %}

## Payment method

You can setup your payment method in the Billing Section of your Team.

On the right side, under the "Summary" section, click on the "Add payment method" link to start.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/add-payment-method.png" caption="Add a payment method" /%}

Upon clicking on the link, you will be requested to enter your credit card information and the email address where you prefer to receive your receipts.

{% callout type="warning" title="Credit card payments" %}
Glide only accepts card payments at this time via Stripe.
{% /callout %}

## Billing history

To access your billing history, head on to the Summary section of your Billing dashboard and click on the "Customer Portal" link. This will redirect you to Stripe where you can view your billing history.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/billing-dashboard.png" caption="Billing dashboard" /%}

This billing history section displays your current or active plans and a list of all your transactions with Glide. You can also view your active payment method here and you can add a new one if necessary.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/billing-history-sample.png" caption="Billing history sample" /%}

### Invoices and receipts

To view your invoices and receipts for a specific transaction, click on the open new tab icon beside the transaction date.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/click-on-icon-to-view-invoice.png" caption="Click on icon to view invoice for that specific transaction" /%}

This will redirect you to the invoice page for that transaction where you can download a PDF copy of your invoice and receipt.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/sample-invoice.png" caption="Sample invoice where you can download a PDF copy of your invoice and receipt" /%}

## Canceling your subscription

Want to downgrade your app from Pro to Free and or change your app subscription? You can do this in your app's billing settings. Just click "Cancel plan."

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/billing-settings.png" caption="Billing settings" /%}

You will then see a modal explaining the changes to your app's plan & features.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/billing/review-subscription-plan-changes.png" caption="Review subscription plan changes" /%}

{% callout type="warning" title="Older subscriptions" %}
If you subscribed before January 2020, your subscription may not show here or may not be associated with your app. If you have any trouble, please open a Support case **here**.
{% /callout %}
