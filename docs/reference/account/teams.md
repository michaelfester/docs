## Creating a team

To create a new team, click on "+ New Team" on the left hand side of your Glide Dashboard. This will prompt a pop-up that will ask you for information about your new team. Fill out the info to add your new team.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/teams/creating-a-team.gif" caption="Creating a team" /%}

## Adding team members

When you create a new team, Glide will prompt you to add your team members during the creation process. However, you can also add members manually once your team has already been created.

### From the Apps section

From the Apps section of your Team folder, you can click on the "Invite Members" button. You can add members by adding their email addresses directly or by sending a link through the "Invite Via Link" option.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/teams/add-team-member-from-apps.gif" caption="Adding members from the Apps section" /%}

### From the Members section

From the Members section of your Team folder, you can also add new members by inputting their email addresses directly or by copying the invite link.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/teams/send-invite-link.png" caption="" /%}

## Deleting a team

To delete a team, go to the billing section of your team folder and click on the three-dot menu on the right-hand corner of the page.

Click on the "Delete Team" option and confirm the pop-up message to proceed with the deletion.

{% figure src="/docs/reference/account/teams/deleting-a-team.gif" caption="Deleting a team" /%}

{% callout type="warning" title="Deleting a team" %}
Deleting a team is a permanent action and cannot be undone. If necessary, make sure you create a back-up of your apps by [transferring them to another team](/docs/projects).
{% /callout %}
