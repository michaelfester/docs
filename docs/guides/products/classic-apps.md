## Classic Apps

Glide Classic Apps is the previous version of the Glide product, which was optimized for mobile and tablet only. It will be available exclusively to users who signed up before March 20, 2023.

We strongly suggest you build new projects on [Apps](/docs/apps), so you can benefit from Glide's new powerful features like actions, integrations and data scalability.

## Migrating from Classic Apps

Classic Apps will continue working and users who signed up before March 2023 can continue building them for now. We are working on a migration plan and will share more details with all relevant users via email and in our community forum.
