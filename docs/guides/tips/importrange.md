**​IMPORTRANGE** is a powerful formula that allows you to bring in cells from other sheets.

{% callout title="" %}
On the free plan, IMPORTRANGE does not update when your sheet is closed. You have to open the importing sheet in your browser for the cells to update; it does not update continuously in the background.
With **Glide Pro** you benefit from the use of **background refresh**.
{% /callout %}
